Correlation plots of the ML-predicted (DFT-computed) $^{129}$Xe magnetic shielding tensor components (a) $\sigma_{xx}^{\text{ML}}$ ($\sigma_{xx}^{\text{DFT}}$), (b) $\sigma_{yy}^{\text{ML}}$ ($\sigma_{yy}^{\text{DFT}}$), (c) $\sigma_{zz}^{\text{ML}}$ ($\sigma_{zz}^{\text{DFT}}$), (d) $\sigma_{xy}^{\text{ML}}$ ($\sigma_{xy}^{\text{DFT}}$), (e) $\sigma_{xz}^{\text{ML}}$ ($\sigma_{xz}^{\text{DFT}}$), and (f) $\sigma_{yz}^{\text{ML}}$ ($\sigma_{yz}^{\text{DFT}}$), from the testing dataset of Xe@CC3@TBA. The dashed line along the diagonal corresponds to $x=y$, while the solid line represents the linear least-squares regression fit. For each plot, the R-squared, RMSE, and MAE values are given in the upper left corner. Histograms along the axes show the projected distributions of ML-predicted and DFT-computed values from the testing dataset.
